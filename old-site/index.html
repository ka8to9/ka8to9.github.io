INTERACTIVE SUSTAINABLE SEAFOOD GUIDE

Client: Seattle Aquarium

"Sustainable seafood is seafood from either fished or farmed sources that can maintain or increase production in the future without jeopardizing the ecosystems from which it was acquired."

This will show not only which seafoods are best choice, good alternative, or need to be avoided, but also sort by type of seafood and water types.

EDUCATIONAL SAKE WEBSITE

Dynamic Website

Client: Japan Prestige Sake Association

Sake has been around for about 2,000 years and has been a unique Japanese tradition and a very big part of their culture. Japan is home to about 2,000 sake breweries or kuramoto (The President of a brewery) and each one prides itself on the distinct local character of their sake.

This is an educational sake website that explains the history of sake and how to enjoy sake in the ways of its cultural atmosphere.

INTERNATIONAL BEER WEB APP

Responsive Design

Client: Seattle/Portland International Beerfest

This web app allows users to type in their favorite beer and it will give them information on the beer such as the label image, name of brewery, location of brewery, and a brief description of the beer itself. It will also let users save their beer search so the information of the beer can be accessed at any time in the future.
